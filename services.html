Agriculture And Farming

Concho Consulting has made its mission about the transformation of Namibian agriculture through earth-conscious and sustainable farming practices. We strive to achieve this mission by working with the principles of Biodynamic Agriculture, and developing educational elements and social life. Consulting focuses on value addition to create wealth at home. The first step consists of interventions to support value addition, upgrading and economic diversification through a needs-oriented and comprehensive approach to industrial development and structural transformation of the Namibian economy towards more productive economic activities.
